# OmniSphere

> Unified Digital Intelligence Platform - The all-in-one SaaS ecosystem that connects automation, analytics, and artificial intelligence.

OmniSphere is a comprehensive platform that combines business automation, analytics, and AI services into a unified ecosystem. The platform is built on a four-layer architecture (Data Ingestion, Processing, Storage, and Delivery) designed for enterprise-grade operations.

Important notes:
- Platform uses a four-layer architecture for data flow
- Supports multiple data sources: APIs, batch upload, streaming (AWS SQS, Active MQ)
- AI credits are consumed per inference and training cycle
- Three pricing tiers available: Starter, Business, and Enterprise
